About

Established in 1991, KOUROSS is a purveyor of both classic and contemporary menswear. Over the years, KOUROSS has carefully curated its selection of brands by prioritising quality and craftsmanship, and offers a well-balanced mix of traditional tailoring, as well as casual staples to suit a variety of occasions.

Suits

Whether you wear suits daily or reserve them for special occasions, at KOUROSS we will be able to assist you in selecting the right one. We offer an extensive ready to wear selection, dependable classics and those on the forefront of the latest style. Our team will be there to walk you through the process and assist you on the journey to selecting the fabric and colour, based on your preference and the purpose, as well as all of the vital aspects to complete your new suit - shirts, shoes, cufflinks and tie clips, pocket squares and socks.

Contemporary Catalogue

KOUROSS caters to the well-dressed gentleman throughout his weekly routine. So, alongside our classic suiting, we provide a range of casual, everyday staples. We have moulded our selection to include a number of brands favoured around the globe for their distinctive design approach and well-made essentials. Our collection has you covered for all occasions, whether there is an after-work outing on the cards or a laid-back weekend excursion.

Made-to-Measure

For almost three decades, we have dedicated ourselves to delivering the perfect suit, so along with the heritage brands that we carry, KOUROSS offers its own solution to the modern man’s tailoring needs. Located on the top floor of each store is our made-to-measure service, which allows our clients to hand-select the different elements of their bespoke suit. Once our team of specialists has taken your measurements, our in-house tailors begin work on a suit that is crafted just for you.
