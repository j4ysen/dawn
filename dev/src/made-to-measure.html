Delivering the perfect suit

Enquire

Once our team of specialists has taken your measurements, our in-house tailors begin work on a suit that is crafted just for you. Time-honoured techniques ensure that it is expertly made and remains in your wardrobe for years to come.

We source exquisite fabrics from a variety of esteemed mills, including Italy’s storied Vitale Barberis Canonico, to ensure that everything we make is durable and drapes just right. Our assortment of textiles allows you to choose from plain and patterned weaves, and permits personalisation, like unique linings and embroidered initials. The customer can choose the details of the garment based on a wide selection of fabric, lapel, lining, buttons, full name or monograms. From Slim Fit and Classic Fit to Taylor Fit, with the fabrics and haute couture materials as required by a fitted suit, jacket, shirt or trousers.

Our experience and knowledge is the most valuable asset that allows us to produce a flawless garment, perfect in every detail.
Each outfit is perfectly tailored for a truly unique experience.

To start your journey towards a perfect suit, you can request an appointment by contacting us. Our team will be in touch in order to walk you through the process and schedule a suitable date.
